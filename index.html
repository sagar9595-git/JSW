From Status To Status Trigger Who Can Transition
Backlog To Do Sprint planning Product Owner / Scrum Master
To Do In Progress Developer picks up ticket Assignee
In Progress In Review PR / MR opened Assignee
In Review In Progress Review requested changes Reviewer
In Review Done PR merged & QA passed Reviewer / QA
In Progress To Do Blocked / deprioritized Assignee / Scrum Master
